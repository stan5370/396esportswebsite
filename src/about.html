About Us

The overall mission of the Esports Club is to provide a community for those interested in any facet of esports and video games at Northwestern.

A key part of this mission is to provide competitive opportunities for those who want to actively compete in esports titles. Opportunities include in-game participation in different competitions, leagues, and practices, as well as out-of-game options such as sports psychology sessions and team bonding activities. It is also the Esports Clubâ€™s goal to ensure that the barrier to participating in these competitive opportunities is as low as possible.

Most important to our mission, however, is fostering a sense of welcome and belonging. It is crucial that those in the Northwestern community see the Esports Club as an open, approachable avenue to explore esports and video games as a whole.
